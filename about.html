Our Story

The desire to bring together flavours and taste from varied cultures to create coffee, drinks and snacks that cater to the vibrant palates of Lagos residents started the Journey that led to the birth of ...Softmeals Chocolate & Coffee Shop.



The essence of Softmeals Chocolate & Coffee Shop is to blend high quality coffee, snacks and exceptional services, typically found in a fine dining establishment, with a casual restaurant arrangement.



To simply put, we are a modern causual restaurant that offer selected but varied choices of different dirinks, pastries, salads and most importantly our refined and quality coffee, in a relaxed and welcoming environment.
